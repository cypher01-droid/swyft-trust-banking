# SwyftTrust

**Secure Managed Banking** — Strategic Banking Like Chess ♞

SwyftTrust is a modern financial platform offering **secure multi-currency management**, **instant loans**, and **real-time tracking** in one unified dashboard.

Experience banking with a grandmaster's touch — combining speed, security, and complete financial control.

---

## ✨ Features

- **Multi-Currency Wallet**  
  Hold and transact in USD, EUR, GBP, and major cryptocurrencies (including BTC) with real-time conversion rates.

- **Instant Deposits & Withdrawals**  
  Fast fund transfers with multi-layer security verification and real-time balance updates.

- **Loan Management**  
  Apply for personal or business loans with competitive rates and flexible repayment terms. Decisions typically within 24-48 hours.

- **Real-Time Refund & Transaction Tracking**  
  Track refunds and transfers using unique tracking codes with live status updates.

- **Complete Transaction History**  
  Full audit trail of all deposits, withdrawals, loans, and transfers.

- **Secure KYC Verification**  
  Quick and automated identity verification process (usually approved within 12-24 hours).

- **Advanced Security**  
  - AES-256 encryption  
  - Multi-factor authentication (MFA)  
  - Strict KYC/AML compliance  
  - **FDIC insured** up to $250,000

- **Global Payment Integrations**  
  Visa, Mastercard, SWIFT, Fedwire, CHIPS, SEPA

## 🚀 How It Works

1. **Open an Account** — Click "Open an Account" and complete the secure registration + KYC upload.
2. **Get Verified** — Most accounts are approved within 12-24 hours.
3. **Fund Your Wallet** — Deposit via supported methods (instant balance update after manual security review).
4. **Manage Finances** — Send/receive money, apply for loans, track refunds — all from one powerful dashboard.
5. **24/7 Support** — Available directly through the platform.
